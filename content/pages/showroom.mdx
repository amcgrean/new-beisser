Our Birchwood / Johnston facility is home to one of the largest **millwork and window and door showrooms** in the region. It is designed to help builders, remodelers, and homeowners see real products, understand their options, and make confident decisions before anything is ordered.

## What You Will Find

- Operating **window and patio door** displays from major brands.
- Interior and exterior door vignettes with real hardware and glass options.
- Millwork walls showing casing, base, crown, and specialty profiles.
- Stair parts, rails, and baluster systems in wood and iron.
- Examples of stain and paint finishes, color combinations, and style packages.

## How Pros Use the Showroom

- Bring clients in to finalize window, door, and trim selections.
- Compare good / better / best options side by side, based on budget.
- Walk through details with our millwork specialists before the order is written.
- Reduce changes and rework by letting customers see and touch real products.

## Location & Hours {#visit}

Treat our showroom like an extension of your own business. If you would like to schedule a dedicated selection appointment, contact our team and we will be glad to help.
