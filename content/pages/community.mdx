Beisser Lumber believes that building a stronger Iowa means investing in the people, programs, and communities that make our state great. Across all locations, we support youth athletics, local nonprofits, civic organizations, hands-on career development programs, and events that bring people together.

Our commitment is rooted in the same values that guide our company: service, integrity, and a belief in doing what is right for our neighbors.

## Key Initiatives

- **Beisser Bowl** – Annual high school football rivalry game between Fort Dodge Senior High and Dallas Center–Grimes, including handcrafted trophies and donations to school booster clubs in the name of each team’s MVP.
- **Habitat for Humanity** – Material donations, volunteer hours, and coordinated build support on projects across the metro.
- **Sleep in Heavenly Peace / Count the Kicks** – Partnerships that support child wellness, safe-sleep education, and SIDS awareness.
- **Build My Future** – Hands-on trades education and career exposure for Iowa students.
- **Local Youth & Civic Programs** – Ongoing support for schools, clubs, alliances, and community organizations in each of our markets.
