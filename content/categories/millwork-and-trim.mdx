Interior doors and trim are where many homeowners really experience the quality of a project. The profiles, reveals, and finish details set the tone for each room and the home as a whole.

## What This Category Covers

- Interior doors in molded, flush, wood, and custom styles.
- Casing, baseboard, crown, and specialty profiles.
- Stair parts, handrail, and balusters.
- Millwork packages tailored to different price points and design styles.

## How Beisser Helps With Interior Doors & Millwork

- **Profile selection:** Our team can suggest standard packages or help create a custom look that fits the home and budget.
- **Shop-built solutions:** With our millwork facility and door plant, we can pre-assemble and label packages to simplify installation on site.
- **Showroom support:** Bring customers to our Birchwood / Johnston showroom to see profiles, doors, and stair parts in person before final decisions are made.
