## Core materials for Iowa builders

Whether you're framing a single-family home, an addition, or a light commercial project, Beisser helps organize lumber and panel lists that keep crews moving and reduce unnecessary site trips.

## Stocked lumber species and sizes

- Fir dimensional lumber
- Whitewood dimensional lumber
- Treated lumber for standard ground-contact and above-grade applications
- Fire-treated lumber
- Cedar
- Pine
- Standard dimensional sizes from **1×4 through 2×12**

## OSB and panel details

Beisser carries **Weyerhaeuser OSB** in thicknesses from **7/16" through 3/4"**. These panels are used for wall and roof sheathing applications, and fire-treatment options are available when the project requires them.
