## Siding choices for Iowa homes

Beisser supports both James Hardie and LP SmartSide so builders can compare weight, install style, finish expectations, and performance based on the home design, crew preference, and budget.

## James Hardie

- **Authorized James Hardie dealer** in Iowa.
- Installed on **more than 10 million homes**.
- Water- and fire-resistant plus insect- and animal-resistant.
- Product formulation is designed for local climate conditions, including Iowa weather swings.
- Available in lap siding, shake, smooth and textured panels, trim, and fascia boards.
- Pre-finished color options are available.

## LP SmartSide

- Lightweight and easy to work with.
- Available in shake, lap siding, panels, trim, and fascia boards.
- Engineered with lumber at its core.
- Built with four protection formulas for impact, wind, fungal rot, and termite resistance.
- Pre-finished color options are available.
