Siding and exterior trim protect the structure, define curb appeal, and influence long-term maintenance. Beisser Lumber works with proven exterior brands so builders can match performance requirements with the look their customers want.

## What This Category Covers

- Fiber cement, vinyl, and engineered wood siding.
- Soffit, fascia, and exterior trim boards.
- Housewrap, flashing, sealants, and related weatherization products.
- Accessory pieces, corners, and details that complete the exterior.

## How Beisser Helps With Siding & Exterior Trim

- **System thinking:** We look at siding, trim, housewrap, flashing, and sealants as a complete system rather than separate items.
- **Brand options:** From James Hardie to LP and other key partners, we can explain where each product fits best.
- **Job planning:** Our team can help plan material orders, deliveries, and stocking strategies so siding crews stay productive on site.
