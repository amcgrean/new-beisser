## Build the whole wall, not just the finish

Beisser Lumber stocks a complete selection of building envelope products for Iowa new construction and renovation — housewrap, insulation, sheathing systems, flashing, and weatherization accessories. These products work together to create an air- and moisture-resistant barrier that protects your structure against Iowa's demanding climate: freeze-thaw cycles, wind-driven rain, and high humidity summers.

## Featured building-envelope brands

### Dow BlueBoard Insulation

- Dow BlueBoard is a closed-cell foam insulation suitable for residential and commercial applications.
- High insulation capacity contributes to energy efficiency.
- Resistant to mold, mildew, and rot.
- Suitable for below-grade, wall, and roof applications.

### DuPont Tyvek

- Industry-standard housewrap creating a wind and moisture barrier.
- Protects sheathing and insulation from exterior moisture intrusion.
- Allows vapor to escape from inside the wall assembly.
- Available in HomeWrap and CommercialWrap variants.

### ZIP System (Huber)

- Structural sheathing with integrated water-resistive barrier.
- ZIP System Tape creates a continuous air barrier at seams.
- Eliminates the need for separate housewrap on many applications.
- High contractor adoption rate in Iowa new construction.
