## Weyerhaeuser partnership and product range

Beisser added Engineered Wood Products in partnership with Weyerhaeuser in 2017, and it has become one of the company's most extensive service lines. The team supports engineered framing packages that reduce guesswork on long spans, open floor plans, and commercial assemblies.

## Components Division: a real differentiator

Beisser's Components Division builds wall panels in a climate-controlled facility with more than 15 years of experience. Panels can be custom-designed for applications ranging from interior walls to finished exterior wall assemblies and arrive ready to install.

## Product support for pros

- Floor trusses and roof trusses coordinated to plan requirements.
- Weyerhaeuser Trus Joist LVL beams, LSL beams, and PSL beams.
- OSB panels, treated beams, and engineered floor systems.
- Wall panelization support for residential, multifamily, and commercial work.
