## Hardware & Screws at Beisser Lumber

Beisser Lumber stocks a complete selection of fasteners, structural connectors, and hardware for interior and exterior applications. Whether you're framing a new home, building a deck, or finishing a room, we carry the hardware your project requires.

### Featured Brand: Simpson Strong-Tie

Simpson Strong-Tie is the industry standard for structural connectors, hangers, and fasteners. Their product line covers joist hangers, post bases, hurricane ties, ledger connectors, and structural screws — essential for code-compliant framing, decking, and load-bearing connections. Available at Beisser locations.

### Door Hardware — Schlage

For door hardware including locksets, deadbolts, and entry hardware, Beisser is a preferred Schlage dealer. Visit our [Doors page](/products/doors) or ask at your branch for Schlage product availability and keying services.

### Interior and Exterior Applications

Hardware and screws are rarely the headline item on a bid, but they matter once materials hit the site. Beisser helps builders and specialty trades coordinate connectors, screws, anchors, and accessory hardware with the framing and exterior systems they are installing — so the right fastener arrives with the right material.
