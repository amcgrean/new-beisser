## Stair Parts at Beisser Lumber

Beisser offers a wide range of stair parts for any style of home — from historical profiles to clean modern designs — suited for both new construction and remodel applications. In addition to classic wood options, baluster choices include vertical balusters and horizontal tubing systems for a more contemporary look.

### What We Carry

**Railings** — Wood and metal railing options for interior stair applications

**Balusters** — Traditional wood balusters, vertical baluster systems, and horizontal tubing for contemporary designs

**Treads** — Stair treads sized for new construction and replacement remodel work

**Posts and Newels** — Coordinated post and newel options to anchor the railing system

**Application:** Suitable for both new construction and remodel stair projects, including historical homes where style matching matters.

### Coordinated Selections

Stair details shape the finished look of a home. Beisser helps tie stair parts into millwork, doors, and trim packages so interior finishes feel coordinated throughout. Ask our team about matching stair selections to your overall interior package — particularly useful for remodelers working room-by-room on existing homes.
