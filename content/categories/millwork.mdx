## Millwork at Beisser Lumber

Beisser carries a complete millwork supply network — from rough-cut material through fine mouldings — covering a wide variety of patterns, styles, and wood species. Whether your project calls for simple painted base or a distinctive crown profile in a natural wood species, our team can source it.

### What We Carry

**Species:** Pine, Oak, Maple, Poplar, MDF

**Finishes:** Unfinished, Primed, Finger-Jointed Primed, and Clay-Coated (select profiles)

**Profile types:** Base, Casing, Crown, Chair Rail, Rosettes

**Availability:** Most popular profiles and sizes are kept in stock. Hundreds of additional profile and species combinations are available within a few days as special orders.

### Featured Brand: Metrie

Metrie is one of North America's leading millwork trim suppliers. Their product line covers a broad range of profiles and finishes designed for both production builders and custom interior work. Available through Beisser Lumber.

### Historical Profile Matching

Our millwork shop can match historical profiles for remodeling projects. If you're working on an older Iowa home and need to match existing trim, ask our team. Beisser's experience with profile matching makes this a practical option for remodelers who need trim that blends seamlessly with the original architecture.
